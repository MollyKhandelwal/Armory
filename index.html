What is the Armory platform?

Armory is a specialized infrastructure for building and deploying custom AI agents. We provide the neural logic and edge nodes required to run autonomous workflows at enterprise scale.

Who is this template designed for?

Armory is designed for engineering teams, AI startups, and enterprise organizations looking to build, deploy, and scale custom AI agents without managing complex infrastructure.

Does Armory provide pre-built agents?

Yes. Armory includes a library of pre-built agent templates for common use cases like customer support, data processing, and content generation. You can customize them or build from scratch.

How does it differ from a standard chatbot?

Unlike simple chatbots, Armory agents can execute multi-step workflows, connect to external APIs, process documents, and make autonomous decisions based on complex conditional logic.

Can I use my own custom domain?

Absolutely. All Armory plans support custom domain configuration. Enterprise plans include dedicated SSL certificates and CDN acceleration for your custom domains.

Is there a limit to how many agents I can build?

Starter plans include 3 active agents, Professional includes 15, and Enterprise plans offer unlimited agents with dedicated infrastructure resources.
